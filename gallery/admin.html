Smile gallery manager

Add or reorder photos, then export gallery.json and any new image files into your gallery/ folder. Replace the existing gallery/gallery.json on your site and upload images to gallery/images/.

Local use only. Do not publish this page to the public web without password protection or your server’s admin panel.

Paths in JSON for images you upload should look like images/yourfile.jpg (inside the gallery/ folder). Remote URLs (https://…) are copied as-is.
