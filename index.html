Our Services

Beautiful, Healthy Smiles —
for Every Age

We care for your family’s smiles—from prevention to cosmetics—with a focus on conservative treatment and patient education.

Foundation of health

Cleanings & Prevention

Exams, cleanings, sealants, fluoride, digital X-rays, and home-care coaching. The ADA recommends regular checkups — we tailor your schedule to your risk and goals so small issues stay small.

Confidence

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, bonding, veneers, lumineers, crowns, bridges, and more — so you can smile freely. We explain options clearly and help you choose what fits your lifestyle and budget.

Healthy gums

Periodontal (Gum) Care

Healthy gums support healthy teeth. We diagnose and treat gum disease, explain causes in plain language, and focus on long-term maintenance so your smile stays stable.

Lasting function

Restorations

Fillings, crowns, bridges, implants, and root canal therapy when needed — always with conservative, tooth-preserving judgment. We want lasting function and a natural look.

Straighter smiles

Orthodontics & Invisalign

Clear aligners and orthodontic care for teens and adults. Straight teeth are easier to clean and can improve bite comfort.

Feel better fast

Dental Pain Relief

Toothaches, sensitivity, jaw pain, or swelling shouldn’t wait. We find the cause of your discomfort, explain it clearly, and treat it with the right care — from fillings and root canal therapy to adjustments for bite or grinding — so you can get out of pain and back to life. Call us if you’re hurting; we’ll prioritize getting you comfortable.

Surgical care

Oral Surgery

Sometimes a tooth needs to be gently removed or a small procedure is needed to keep your mouth healthy. We provide comfortable, in-office care, including extractions and wisdom teeth when needed. We’ll clearly explain your comfort options and give simple aftercare instructions to help you heal smoothly.

Permanent replacement

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a strong, natural-looking way to replace missing teeth. We evaluate your bone and gums, walk you through each step, and focus on long-term success — from single-tooth replacement to full-arch options when appropriate.

Comfort & function

Dentures

Custom full and partial dentures to replace missing teeth, restore chewing comfort, and help you smile with confidence.

Precision & clarity

Digital Technologies

We use digital X-rays for clear, low-radiation imaging and fast diagnosis, and intraoral scanners for comfortable digital impressions — no messy putty — so treatment planning is accurate and your visits stay efficient.

We’re here for you

Urgent & Emergency Care

Tooth pain, broken teeth, or swelling? Call right away — we’ll advise you on next steps and get you seen as soon as we can.

Our Practice

Meet Dr. Sachdev

This practice has been serving the community for over 20 years.

Dr. Sachdev is an accomplished dentist, with her dental education right here in Long Island at Stony Brook University, residency at Lutheran Hospital, followed by hundreds of hours of continuing education focused on cutting-edge advances in the world of dentistry and patient care.

Dr. Sachdev was also a teacher and an attending dentist/supervisor at Brookdale Hospital in Brooklyn, where she helped dental residents hone their clinical and patient management skills, and helped ensure that the next generation of dentists are not only proficient in dental procedures but also in human aspect of dental care.

Dr. Sachdev is a 4th generation dentist in her family, with her great grandfather being the first dentist from London, then her grandfather and her father. In India, her family has been known for practicing state-of-the-art dentistry, serving senior government officials and other celebrities. She brings the same dedication and professionalism to serve her patients in Long Island, as her great grandfather did 80–90 years ago.

Dr. Sachdev and her staff’s primary focus is on prevention and minimizing the need for complex dental procedures. She works with her patients to establish a comprehensive treatment plan that incorporates the patient’s dental needs and their economic situation, with the view to maximize the benefits of insurance coverage today while minimizing the need for complex expensive dental procedures tomorrow.
